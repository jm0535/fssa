# FSSA — Forestry Students & Staff Association

Official website for the **Forestry Students & Staff Association (FSSA)**, School of Forestry, Faculty of Natural Resources, Papua New Guinea University of Technology (PNGUoT).

🌐 **Live site:** [jm0535.github.io/fssa](https://jm0535.github.io/fssa/)

## About

The FSSA represents students and staff across the School of Forestry's three campuses:

- **Taraka Campus** — Main School of Forestry (Lae, Morobe Province)
- **Bulolo University College (BUC)** — Bulolo, Morobe Province
- **Timber & Forestry Training College (TFTC)** — Bumbu, Lae

The site showcases FSSA leadership, academic programs, research themes, events, and contact information.

## Tech Stack

- HTML5, CSS3, vanilla JavaScript
- Google Fonts (Playfair Display + Inter)
- Responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Scroll-based reveal animations
- GitHub Pages hosting
