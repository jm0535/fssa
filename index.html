About FSSA

The Forestry Students & Staff Association (FSSA) is the heart and soul of the School of Forestry at Papua New Guinea University of Technology (PNGUoT).

We are a vibrant community that unites students and staff under one banner — the Diwai Family. Our association fosters academic excellence, professional development, and cultural unity among forestry students across all year levels.

The School of Forestry (SoF), within the Faculty of Natural Resources, has built a strong reputation for providing exceptional training in tropical forestry. Situated on the traditional lands of the Ahi people, SoF operates across three campuses: Taraka (main campus), Bulolo University College (BUC), and the Timber & Forestry Training College (TFTC) at Bumbu — which joined the School in 2023. With 36 academic staff and active research collaborations with CIFOR-ICRAF, IPB University (Indonesia), University of Melbourne (Australia), and Harvard University, the School is a regional leader in forestry education.

Our Programs

The premier pathway for training professional foresters in Papua New Guinea

Undergraduate

Bachelor of Science in Forestry (BScF)

A comprehensive 4-year degree program at Taraka campus covering forest management, conservation, ecology, forest engineering, and environmental science. In 2025, 42 students presented final-year research projects spanning diverse topics.

  • 4-year full-time program
  • BScF Year 1 through Year 4
  • Final Year Research Project (FR421)
  • Industry work experience (PNGFIA MoU)
  • International accreditation benchmarking with University of Canterbury, NZ
Taraka Campus
Postgraduate

MPhil & PhD in Forestry

In 2025, SoF had 13 active postgraduate students (4 PhD, 9 MPhil) conducting cutting-edge research across biodiversity, carbon science, wood technology, and conservation ecology.

  • Master of Philosophy (MPhil)
  • Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)
  • 13 active postgrad students (2025)
  • CIFOR-ICRAF & EU-FCCB scholarships
Bulolo Campus (BUC)

Diploma in Forestry & BFRM

Bulolo University College offers a 3-year Diploma in Forestry (DipFor) and has expanded with a Bachelor of Forest Resource Management (BFRM) program since 2023.

  • 3-year Diploma in Forestry (DipFor)
  • Bachelor of Forest Resource Management (BFRM)
  • Field station & practical training
Bulolo University College
Bumbu Campus (TFTC)

Diploma in Wood Science & Technology

The Timber & Forestry Training College (TFTC) joined SoF in 2023, offering hands-on vocational training in sawmilling, cabinet making, wood machining, and timber processing.

  • 2-year Diploma program
  • Sawmilling & wood machining
  • Cabinet making & joinery
  • Saw doctoring & timber grading
Bumbu Campus (TFTC)
